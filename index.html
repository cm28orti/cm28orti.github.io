My fav things

Exploring My City

I love where I come from, The Big Apple. There's so much to do besides the typical tourist activities and I thrive on exploring all of the many unknown wonders NYC has to offer.

Queen Bey

I love Beyonce. She has been one of my role models from a young age. She's an advocate for women empowerment, self love, independence, making an impacts and appreciating all experiences no matter the obstacles that come with it all.

My Bros

I am the only girl in a household with four males. My brothers are the most important people in my life. I wouldn't be the person I am today without them and all that they have taught me.
